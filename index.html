Thanks for finding my little corner of the internet.

I'm currently an undergraduate Computer Engineering student at Michigan Technological University (go Huskies!). In my free time I love the outdoors, exploring the Keweenaw Penninsula, and much more. I'm also an active member of the Rowing Club at Michigan Tech (pictured below).

I first got into programming from a young age, getting involved with First Lego League robotics, and eventually moving into the VEX Robotics Competition. Through these experiences I learned that I really enjoyed programming these robots, and began doing small projects on the side, and learning programming from various books. My first website I ever built that went live was a small site built with a close friend for a robotics team at the time. Though rather ugly and completely unoptimized for mobile devices, it was a fun experience and a big accomplishment for us as 8th graders.
Since then, I've worked on a variety of projects, usually finding a way to work in a programming project for a school project (because it's "homework", but fun) until eventually enrolling here at Michigan Tech.
